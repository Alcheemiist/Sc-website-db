Employment:

                            Full Time
                        



-	According to brand positioning, product positioning, user demand scenarios and industry hotspots, planning creative activities and promotional materials to attract users;
-	Responsible for the social marketing communication plan of major company-level projects and activities, internally connect with relevant departments, create various cooperation values, and grasp externally various platform traffic bonuses to expand influence;
-	Responsible for online planning and promotion plans, analyzing and grasping the needs of users and customers, adjusting online platform content according to needs, and grasping the overall style and development direction;
-	Track daily data and Marketing effect data, analyzing social media operation indicators, discovering customer needs, optimizing and adjusting marketing strategies in a timely manner, and being responsible for online marketing effects.

REQUIREMENTS
-	Bachelor degree or above, more than three years experience in Internet company social media operations, Property Finder, Airbnb work experience is preferred.
-	Participated in and led the project planning, system construction, and specific operation and implementation of new media businesses or related businesses, and has rich experience in data dissemination, community operation and event planning;
-	Familiar with the characteristics and ecology of Internet self-media platforms , gameplay, there are successful cases in the operation of the "traffic pool";
-	Have a good teamwork spirit and innovation ability, and be able to complete the operation work independently;
-	Have a relatively comprehensive planning ability, familiar with event marketing and social marketing
-	Strong sense of responsibility for work, able to continuously learn new knowledge, have independent thinking and work quality, and can withstand greater work pressure.






ABOUT THE COMPANY
METAHOMES, established in 2022 and owned by Metaworld Group which the headquarters locate in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, is a global travel experience and real estate transaction platform with advanced and creative technologies. It’s dedicated to innovate the investment and transactions for the real estate industry by providing the best experience for global travelers and transaction services for our customers all over the world, helping our customers to obtain the largest benefit from real estate investment with creative technologies.
METAHOMES provides products and services including the search online and reservation of homestays, hotels, holiday houses, apartments, etc. Meanwhile, METAHOMES also provides global transaction services in real estate market by helping our customers to search and transact the properties. METAHOMES will use advanced VR, AI, IOT technologies to provide our customers with immersive experience, imitating the real experience during the property tour and provide the full intelligent services when our customers are dealing with the transactions in real estate market that can let you easily find your favorited accommodations or the inspiration for the future reservations.
