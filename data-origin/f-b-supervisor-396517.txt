Our client is currently seeking a dedicated and skilled F&B Supervisor to join their dynamic team. This role is pivotal in ensuring exceptional dining experiences for guests and maintaining the highest standards of service excellence.

Key Responsibilities:

-	The F&B Supervisor will be responsible for overseeing daily F&B operations, guaranteeing top-notch service and customer satisfaction.
-	They will play a crucial role in mentoring and leading a team of service staff, setting and maintaining high-performance standards.
-	Collaborating closely with the kitchen team to ensure food quality and consistency will be one of their key responsibilities.
-	Monitoring inventory levels and placing orders to ensure a seamless dining service is essential.
-	Handling guest inquiries, requests, and concerns, and addressing issues promptly and professionally is crucial for this role.
-	Maintaining cleanliness and hygiene standards in dining areas, in compliance with health and safety regulations, will be part of their daily routine.
-	Additionally, they will assist in creating and implementing promotions and special events to boost revenue and enhance customer engagement.


We request that candidates send their CV as a Microsoft Word document where possible.

Quest Search and Selection is acting as an Employment Agency in relation to this vacancy.







REQUIREMENTS
Are you the dedicated and passionate F&B Supervisor our client is looking for? Here's what the perfect candidate profile should entail:

 - Experience: Demonstrated experience in F&B operations, preferably with supervisory or leadership roles.
 - Leadership Skills: Strong leadership abilities to motivate and manage a team effectively.
 - Customer Focus: A customer-centric mindset with the ability to create exceptional dining experiences.
 - Communication: Excellent communication skills, both with guests and team members.
 - Problem-Solving: Proven ability to handle guest inquiries, complaints, and resolve issues efficiently.
 - Attention to Detail: Keen eye for detail to maintain high standards of service and hygiene.
 - Adaptability: Flexibility to work in a dynamic and fast-paced F&B environment.

If you embody these qualities and are ready to take on this exciting opportunity, apply now to join our client's team as an F&B Supervisor!
