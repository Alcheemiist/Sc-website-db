Talent Analytics Senior Specialist for International Consulting Firm, Dubai

The role is responsible for supporting the talent analytics agenda including implementing data- driven solutions, developing use cases and uncovering insights. The role is also responsible for supporting the delivery of talent analytics projects and its related operations.

Responsibilities:
•	Contribute to the development of a library of data-driven talent related use cases (incl. conducting research, documenting findings, preparing presentations)
•	Support in the development and maintenance of a comprehensive set of key performance indicators (KPIs) in line with the identified use cases
•	Collaborate closely with relevant departments to implement cross functional data driven use cases.
•	Collect, clean, and structure data from various sources.
•	Continuously monitor and ensure the quality, accuracy and integrity of the data, identifying and resolving data quality issues
•	Analyze the data by applying statistical and data analysis techniques to uncover patterns, trends, and correlations and to achieve the use case objectives.
•	Develop predictive models and algorithms to forecast talent-related trends.
•	Design and build visually engaging and informative performance management dashboards for business stakeholders, facilitating data-driven decision-making.
•	Generate regular and ad-hoc reports to communicate data findings to relevant stakeholders.
•	Collaborate with the digital department to integrate systems and technologies, that support interdepartmental use cases and ensure seamless data flow
•	Support the Talent Analytics Senior Manager in delivering talent analytics projects, in close coordination with the Digital department.
•	Coordinate and liaise with product developers and relevant project managers (e.g., Emtech team, external developers)
•	Support in testing newly developed tools and related add-ons







REQUIREMENTS
Skills:
•	Bachelor’s Degree, Masters is a plus
•	With 2-4 years of experience in people analytics, business analytics or data analytics (experience in consulting and/or talent development is a plus)
•	Strong oral and written communication skills as well as presentation skills
•	Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a keen eye for detail
•	Ability to crunch and interpret complex data and draw actionable insights and recommendations.
•	Proficiency in data analytics tools and programming languages like Python, R, SQL, and data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or others
•	Service-oriented attitude, proactive thinker, information seeker, team player
•	Ability to work within an unstructured fast paced environment.
•	Advanced knowledge of Excel and PowerPoint
•	Knowledge of HR processes is a plus
