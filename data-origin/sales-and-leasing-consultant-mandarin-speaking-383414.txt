Employment:

Are you an experienced Sales professional with the ability to speak Mandarin interested in earning a tax-free salary?

If so, we are currently looking for such an individual to join our clients, a well-established and expanding property company in Dubai.

Our clients are happy to consider experienced property professionals or those looking to get into the industry with considerable sales/customer service industry experience.

This position is a rare opportunity to join one of the top real estate companies in the UAE where you will be supported by a strong Senior team, including the owner of the business, with commissions paid weekly.

Main Responsibilities:

- Booking and attending Viewings
- Booking and attending Valuations
- Negotiating on Properties

Full Package:

- On target earnings 20,000 - 40,000 AED monthly
- TAX FREE
- 50% Commission on properties let or sold
- Strong internal training - dedicated Training Academy and trainer 
- Working Visa
- Weekly pay
- Close team environment and team-building activities 
- Full support from management 
- A modern and well-designed office

Salary:

                            AED

                                                                                                    per month inclusive of fixed allowances.

Experience:

- Passion for Property
- Must be able to speak Mandarin
- Strong negotiating skills and the ability to close for business
- Excellent Customer service
