Employment:

                            Full Time
                        



We are seeking a reliable and responsible individual to fill the role of a Family Driver. The ideal candidate will be tasked with ensuring the safe and efficient transportation of family members to various destinations, while maintaining a high level of professionalism, discretion, and punctuality.

Responsibilities:

Safe Transportation: Safely drive family members to and from destinations, including but not limited to schools, appointments, and social activities. Adhere to all traffic rules and regulations to ensure the safety of passengers.

Vehicle Maintenance: Conduct regular maintenance checks on the assigned vehicle, including fueling, oil changes, and tire rotations. Report any mechanical issues promptly and ensure necessary repairs are carried out.

Punctuality: Maintain a high level of punctuality to ensure timely arrival at scheduled destinations. Plan routes effectively to avoid delays and optimize travel time.

Professionalism: Demonstrate a professional and courteous demeanor at all times. Ensure the cleanliness and tidiness of the vehicle both inside and outside.

Security and Safety: Implement safety measures, such as ensuring all passengers wear seatbelts. Be vigilant and aware of the surroundings to address any potential security concerns.

Communication: Maintain clear and open communication with the family regarding scheduling and any changes to the daily itinerary. Report any incidents, accidents, or concerns promptly.

Flexibility: Be adaptable to changes in schedule or additional responsibilities as required. Assist with errands or tasks as requested by the family.

Salary:

                            AED
                                                                                                            4,000 to 4,500
                                                                                                    per month inclusive of fixed allowances.
                            
Additional benefits: visa and medical insurance








REQUIREMENTS
Requirements:
-	UAE Valid driver's license with a clean driving record.
-	Proven experience as a personal or family driver.
-	Familiarity with local traffic rules and regulations.
-	Excellent navigation skills.
-	Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
-	Punctual and reliable with a high level of integrity.
-	Ability to maintain confidentiality and exercise discretion.
-	Physical fitness and ability to perform basic vehicle maintenance tasks.

Availability:
Preference will be given to candidates available immediately
