Employment:

                            Internship
                        



We are seeking two highly motivated and creative Social Media Interns to join our dynamic marketing team. As a Social Media Intern, you will play a crucial role in promoting our brand and engaging our audience through captivating videos and posts that align with our brand guidelines. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in social media marketing and video production.

Responsibilities:
•	Video Shoot & Editing: Collaborate with the marketing team to conceptualize, plan, and execute engaging video content for our social media platforms. Utilize your video shooting and editing skills to create captivating and high-quality content.
•	Post Creation: Craft visually appealing and engaging social media posts that align with our brand guidelines. Use creativity to drive brand awareness and connect with our target audience effectively.
•	Brand Consistency: Ensure all social media content adheres to our brand guidelines, maintaining a cohesive and recognizable brand identity across all platforms.
•	Community Engagement: Engage with our social media followers, respond to comments, and foster meaningful interactions to enhance brand loyalty and build a strong online community.
•	Analytics & Insights: Monitor social media analytics to track the performance of content and campaigns. Utilize data to identify trends and make data-driven decisions to improve engagement.







REQUIREMENTS
Requirements:
•	Outstanding skills in video shooting and editing, using software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or other relevant tools.
•	Proficiency in creating visually appealing and engaging social media content, with a keen eye for design aesthetics.
•	Solid understanding of various social media platforms, their audiences, and trends.
•	Strong knowledge of brand guidelines and the ability to adapt creatively while staying within the brand’s visual identity.
•	Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
•	A passion for social media marketing, staying up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices.
•	Creativity, innovation, and the ability to think outside the box to develop fresh and exciting content.
•	Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines effectively.
•	Availability to work ( Monday to Friday 9AM - 6PM)


If you are a proactive and talented individual with a passion for social media and a flair for creating compelling content, we encourage you to apply for this internship. Join us in shaping our brand’s online presence and making a meaningful impact in the digital landscape.

Note: As an intern, you will receive mentorship and guidance from our experienced marketing team while having the chance to showcase your skills and creativity. This internship is a 3 month commitment, and we welcome candidates from diverse backgrounds.







ABOUT THE COMPANY
Avyanco emerged from the ambition of aiding upcoming entrepreneurs and companies to set up their business in the UAE. Offering them complete business set up, accounting, auditing and taxation solutions. Our mission and values are infused with the core principles of productivity, innovation, transparency, trust, respect, and responsibility for one’s actions which embody the collective conscience of the company and its people. We are a hub for business setup in Dubai and UAE.
